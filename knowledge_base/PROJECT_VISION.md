## 1. Brand Identity
- **Principal:** Acharya Rajesh 
- **Title:**  Astrologer
- **Core Value:** Scholarly, authoritative, and traditional  wisdom delivered through modern technology.
- **Primary Language:** Hindi (Long-form blogs).
- **Secondary Language:** English (AI-generated translations and summaries).

## 2. Business Objectives
- **Authority Building:** Establish Acharya Rajesh as a thought leader via deep-dive astrological analysis.
- **Lead Generation:** Convert blog readers into consultation clients.
- **Content Protection:** Protect original Hindi research from easy digital theft/copying.
- **Global Reach:** Use AI to make Hindi content accessible to English-speaking audiences.

## 3. Primary User Journeys
1. **The Reader:** Lands on a Hindi blog via search or social media -> Reads deep content -> Views AI summary in English -> Sees "Important Dates" -> Clicks CTA "Book an Appointment."
2. **The Client:** Navigates directly to the booking page -> Authenticates via Google -> Selects a slot -> Receives confirmation.
3. **The Admin (Acharya Rajesh):** Logs in -> Writes blog in Hindi -> Triggers AI tools (Translate/Summarize/Extract) -> Reviews and publishes.

## 4. Visual Language (Based on Logo)
- **Primary Color:** Navy Blue (Authority, Depth, Night Sky).
- **Accent Color:** Gold (Wisdom, Purity, Solar energy).
- **Typography:** Serif (Martel for Hindi, Newsreader for English) to evoke a "Journal" feel.
